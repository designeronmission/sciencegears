Refund Policy

Our policy on returns and refunds for your purchases

Refund Policy Sections

Overview Eligibility Timeframe Return Process Refund Methods Non-Refundable Damaged Items Shipping Costs Exchanges Contact Us
Last Updated:

1. Overview

At ScienceGears.com.au, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not satisfied, we're happy to offer refunds or exchanges on most products within a specified period, subject to the conditions outlined in this policy.

Key Point: Please inspect your order upon receipt and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged, or if you receive the wrong item.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a return and refund, your item must meet the following criteria:

Approved Returns Approved
  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Within the return timeframe (see Section 3)
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase
Denied Returns Denied
  • Used or damaged items
  • Items not in original condition
  • Perishable or personalized goods
  • Hazardous materials

3. Return Timeframe

Our standard return and refund periods are as follows:

Product Category Return Period Refund Processing
Standard Products 30 days from delivery 5-10 business days
Electronics 14 days from delivery 7-14 business days
Special Orders Case by case Upon approval
Clearance Items Final sale (no returns) N/A
The return period is calculated from the date you receive your order (or the date the carrier confirms delivery).

4. Return Process

Follow these steps to initiate a return:

1
Contact Us

Email returns@sciencegears.com.au with your order number and reason for return

2
Get Approval

Wait for our return authorization (typically within 2 business days)

3
Ship Item Back

Package securely and ship to provided address with tracking

Important: Returns sent without prior authorization may not be accepted. Please include the original packing slip or a copy of your order confirmation in your return package.

5. Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase, unless otherwise specified:

Standard Refunds
  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days after processing
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days after processing
  • Bank Transfer: 7-14 business days after processing
Store Credit
  • May be offered in some circumstances
  • Does not expire
  • Can be used for future purchases
  • Issued immediately via email

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for returns or refunds unless defective:

  • Downloadable software products
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Perishable goods (chemicals, specimens)
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Gift cards
  • Items marked as non-returnable

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately:

Required Information
  • Order number
  • Photos of damaged item
  • Photos of packaging
  • Description of issue
Our Response
  • Typically within 2 business days
  • Replacement or refund options
  • Prepaid return label if needed
  • No restocking fee

8. Shipping Costs

Our policy on return shipping costs:

We Cover Shipping
  • For damaged/defective items
  • For incorrect items shipped
  • When return is our error
Customer Covers Shipping
  • For change of mind returns
  • For non-defective returns
  • Original shipping costs non-refundable

9. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for most items within 30 days of delivery:

Exchange Process
  • Contact us to initiate exchange
  • Return original item per return policy
  • Once received, we'll ship replacement
  • Price differences will be adjusted
Exchange Notes
  • Subject to product availability
  • Customer pays return shipping
  • We cover shipping for replacement
  • Faster option: refund and new order
