Squidstat Plus

±1 A Potentiostat with EIS up to 2 MHz and 1% / 2° Accuracy at 1 mΩ

4.8 (42 reviews)

In stock, ready to ship

The Squidstat Plus is a high-performance potentiostat with electrochemical impedance spectroscopy (EIS) capabilities up to 2 MHz. With exceptional 1% / 2° accuracy at 1 mΩ, it's the ideal solution for advanced electrochemical research and quality control applications.

Technical Specifications

Precision engineering for accurate measurements

Voltage Scan Range ±10 V
Max Current ±1 A
EIS Frequency Range Up to 2 MHz
Best Current Accuracy 1 nA
Channels Per Unit 1
Input Impedance > 10¹² Ω
Potential Resolution 300 µV
Current Resolution < 750 fA

Performance Highlights

High Speed Data Acquisition

16-bit resolution at 1 MS/s sampling rate for capturing fast transient events.

Advanced EIS Capabilities

2 MHz frequency range with 1% / 2° accuracy at 1 mΩ for detailed impedance analysis.

Precision Electronics

Low-noise design with < 750 fA current resolution for sensitive measurements.

Why Choose Squidstat Plus?

Advanced features for professional electrochemical research

Multichannel System

Connect multiple units for parallel experiments and increased throughput.

Onboard Memory

16GB memory ensures no data loss even during computer issues.

Real-Time Adjustments

Modify parameters during experiments for maximum flexibility.

Drag & Drop Builder

Create custom experiments without coding skills required.
