How do I track my order? +

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can enter this number on our website's tracking page or on the courier's website to see the current status and estimated delivery date of your package.

What is your return policy? +

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most unopened and unused items. Laboratory equipment and chemicals may have different return policies due to safety regulations. Please contact our support team before initiating a return for these items.

How do I calibrate my laboratory scale? +

Calibration procedures vary by model. Please refer to the user manual that came with your scale or download it from our product support page. For professional calibration services, we recommend contacting an accredited calibration laboratory.

Do you offer educational discounts? +

Yes, we offer discounts for educational institutions, teachers, and students. Please register with your educational email address or contact our sales team with proof of your educational status to receive discount information.

What safety certifications do your products have? +

Our products meet various international safety standards depending on the product type. Common certifications include CE, UL, ISO, and ASTM standards. Specific certification information is available on each product page and in the product documentation.
