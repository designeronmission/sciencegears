Electrochemical Methods/Techniques

Cyclic Voltammetry (CV)

Measure current while cycling the potential of a working electrode at a fixed rate between set limits. Our system provides ultra-precise control for accurate CV measurements.

Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS)

Measure impedance over a range of frequencies to characterize electrochemical systems with our advanced EIS capabilities.

Chronoamperometry (CA)

Measure current over time at a fixed potential to study reaction kinetics with high temporal resolution.

Potentiostatic/Galvanostatic

Control either potential or current while measuring the other parameter with our dual-mode system.
