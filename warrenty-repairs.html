Warranty & Repairs

Sciencegears Limited Warranty and Repair Services for your Potentiostat

Limited Warranty Coverage COVERED

Sciencegears warrants that your potentiostat will be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a period of 24 months from the date of purchase.

Warranty Period:
  • Main unit: 24 months
  • Electrodes and consumables: 6 months
  • Software: 12 months (updates included)

What's Covered:

  • Defects in materials or workmanship
  • Manufacturing faults
  • Software malfunctions
  • Component failures under normal use

What's Not Covered NOT COVERED

The warranty does not cover damage resulting from:

  • Accidental damage or improper use
  • Exposure to liquids or corrosive chemicals
  • Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Use with incompatible accessories
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Acts of nature (floods, fires, etc.)

Warranty Terms & Conditions

Condition Details

Repair Process

Follow these steps to request warranty service or repairs:

Diagnose the Issue

Consult the troubleshooting guide in your manual or contact our technical support team for preliminary diagnosis.

Obtain RMA Number

Contact Sciencegears support to receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. No returns accepted without RMA.

Package Your Unit

Securely pack the unit in its original packaging if possible. Include all accessories and a copy of your purchase receipt.

Ship to Service Center

Send to the authorized service center indicated in your RMA documentation. Customer pays shipping to center.

Repair & Return

Typical repair turnaround is 10-15 business days after receipt. Expedited service available for additional fee.

Important: Any attempt to repair the unit yourself will void the warranty. All warranty service must be performed by authorized Sciencegears technicians.
